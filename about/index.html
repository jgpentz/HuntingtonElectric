Our Vision

We go above and beyond every day to make sure that every name in our system is that of a happy and satisfied customer. A customer that feels comfortable and safe after our work is done. We are fueled by our loyal customers and the new ones we make everyday. We see how other companies operate and always strive to do better. It's more than just a smiling face, It's also quality work that we provide that keeps your house running safely and consistently.

Our Story

In 1993 our President and Founder, Blair Huntington, saw how other companies operated and worked through many of them, seeing an opportunity to provide a higher quality of service that would make customers happier than they have ever been with an electrician. We didn't want to be another service guy to walk through your doors, we are different, Northern Virginia is our family.
