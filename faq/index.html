FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

What are your earliest and latest appointment times?

Our first call of each day is to arrive between 7-7:30am and our last call window is typically 1-3pm.

What payment types do you accept?

We accept Cash, Checks, VISA and MasterCard.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! we are both licensed and insured.

Are estimates provided for free? Can you give estimates over the phone?

Yes, estimates are free! We will take your information and have our estimator set up a time with you to come and take a look at your project. Over the phone we can provide ballparks for smaller jobs but it's hard to tell without seeing it, and bigger jobs will typically require an estimator visiting. Click here to contact us and get started!

How far out are you with scheduling?

We are usually roughly 2 weeks out for scheduling, but openings are always popping up. Give us a call and we can work with you if you have an emergency or need something ASAP.
